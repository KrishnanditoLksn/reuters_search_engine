Isi: If you want to be the host with the most this spring by presenting your potluck in cheery dishware, Great Jones just came in clutch. The Striped ceramic collection refreshes the brand’s bestsellers, including the Hot Dish, the Little Hottie and the Stir Crazy, starting at $60.
