Judul: ‘Suppressing essential health information’: Some officials frustrated by Trump administration’s limits on health agencies
Wartawan: Curt Devine, Majlie de Puy Kamp and Audrey Ash,
Isi Berita: 
Communication Restrictions: The Trump administration has directed federal health agencies to pause external communications, including scientific reports, website updates, and health advisories, leading to concerns among officials about the timely dissemination of critical health information. ​
Impact on Public Awareness: These limitations hinder the public's access to essential health updates, potentially affecting awareness and response to health issues.​
Internal Frustrations: Some officials within health agencies express frustration over these restrictions, fearing they may impede the agencies' ability to effectively inform and protect the public.​
These developments have raised concerns about the transparency and effectiveness of health communications under the current administration.
