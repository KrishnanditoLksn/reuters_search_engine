Judul: Illnesses from bacteria in food are on the rise. A doctor explains how to avoid them
Wartawan:  Katia Hetter
Isi Berita: 
E. coli bacteria can cause severe gastrointestinal illnesses, including symptoms like diarrhea, abdominal cramps, and vomiting. To minimize the risk of infection, it's crucial to practice proper food safety measures. This includes thoroughly cooking meats, particularly ground beef, to safe internal temperatures, as undercooked meats have been linked to outbreaks. Additionally, washing fruits and vegetables under running water before consumption can help remove potential contaminants. Maintaining good hygiene, such as regular handwashing with soap and water, especially before handling food, is also essential. By adhering to these practices, individuals can significantly reduce the risk of E. coli infections and promote overall health
