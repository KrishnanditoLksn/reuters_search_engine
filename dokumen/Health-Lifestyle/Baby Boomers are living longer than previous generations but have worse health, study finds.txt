Judul: Baby Boomers are living longer than previous generations but have worse health, study finds
Wartawan:  Rosa Rahimi
Isi Berita: 
While life expectancy has increased over the decades, studies indicate that baby boomers are experiencing more health issues compared to previous generations. Factors such as higher rates of obesity, sedentary lifestyles, and increased alcohol consumption contribute to chronic conditions like diabetes, heart disease, and liver problems among this demographic. Addressing these challenges requires a focus on preventive healthcare measures, including regular physical activity, balanced nutrition, and moderation in alcohol intake, to improve the quality of life as individuals age
