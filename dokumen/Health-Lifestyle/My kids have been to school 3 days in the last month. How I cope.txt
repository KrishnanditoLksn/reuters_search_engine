Judul: My kids have been to school 3 days in the last month. How I cope
Wartawan: Kara Alaimo
Isi Berita: 
Challenges for Working Parents: Balancing job responsibilities with caring for a sick child presents significant difficulties, especially for those without flexible work arrangements or paid leave.​
Lack of Paid Sick Leave: Many U.S. workers lack access to paid sick leave, forcing tough choices between income and caregiving. ​
Impact on Child Health: Sending ill children to school or daycare can worsen their condition and spread illness.​
Potential Solutions: Advocating for universal paid family and medical leave policies could support families during health crises. ​
Addressing these challenges requires policy changes and supportive workplace cultures to ensure parents can care for their children without compromising their livelihoods
