Judul: Are you worried about your child’s size? Here’s why it may not be an issue
Wartawan: Madeline Holcombe
Isi Berita: 
Assessing children's body size is crucial for monitoring their health and development. However, reliance solely on metrics like Body Mass Index (BMI) can be misleading, as it doesn't account for factors like muscle mass, bone density, and individual growth patterns. Misinterpretation of such metrics can lead to unnecessary concerns or overlooked health issues. It's essential for healthcare providers to consider a holistic approach, evaluating diet, physical activity, and overall well-being alongside standard measurements to ensure accurate health assessments for children.
