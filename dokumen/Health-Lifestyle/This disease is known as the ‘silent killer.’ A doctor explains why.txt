Judul: This disease is known as the ‘silent killer.’ A doctor explains why
Wartawan:Katia Hetter
Isi Berita: 
Global Increase in Chronic Diseases: Chronic conditions such as hypertension and diabetes are escalating worldwide, posing significant health challenges.​
Contributing Factors: The rise in these diseases is linked to factors like poor diet, lack of physical activity, and increased consumption of processed foods.​ Impact on Healthcare Systems: The growing prevalence of chronic diseases places a substantial burden on healthcare infrastructures globally.​
Preventive Measures: Adopting healthier lifestyles, including balanced nutrition and regular exercise, is crucial in combating these conditions.
