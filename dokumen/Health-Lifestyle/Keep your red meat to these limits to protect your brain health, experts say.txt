Judul: Keep your red meat to these limits to protect your brain health, experts say
Wartawan: Madeline Holcombe
Isi Berita: 
A study suggests that consuming red and processed meat may increase the risk of various health issues, including cancer, heart disease, and dementia. Researchers emphasize that a high intake of these meats is linked to cognitive decline over time.
To reduce the risk of dementia, experts recommend limiting red and processed meat consumption and increasing the intake of flavonoid-rich foods, such as berries, leafy greens, dark chocolate, and red wine. A balanced diet and a healthy lifestyle play a crucial role in maintaining brain health and preventing cognitive decline.
