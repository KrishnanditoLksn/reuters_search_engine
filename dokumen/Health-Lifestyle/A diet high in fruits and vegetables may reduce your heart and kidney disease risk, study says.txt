Judul: A diet high in fruits and vegetables may reduce your heart and kidney disease risk, study says
Wartawan: Madeline Holcombe
Isi Berita: 
A diet rich in fruits and vegetables is associated with a lower risk of developing heart and kidney diseases. These foods provide essential nutrients, antioxidants, and fiber that contribute to cardiovascular health and aid in maintaining optimal kidney function. Health professionals recommend integrating a variety of fruits and vegetables into daily meals to promote overall well-being and reduce the likelihood of chronic diseases.
