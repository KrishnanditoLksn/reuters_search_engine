Judul: One small thing you can do for healthier food and exercise over holiday travel
Wartawan: Madeline Holcombe
Isi Berita: 
Holiday travel can be stressful and expensive, but with advance planning, it's more manageable. Experts recommend booking flights early, considering alternate airports, and traveling during off-peak times to save money. Staying informed about airline policies, packing essentials like noise-canceling headphones and snacks, and allowing extra time for security can also ease the journey. Maintaining health during travel involves staying hydrated, moving regularly during long flights, and practicing good hygiene. By implementing these strategies, travelers can enjoy a smoother and more pleasant holiday experience.
