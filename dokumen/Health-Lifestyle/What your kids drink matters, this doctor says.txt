Judul: What your kids drink matters, this doctor says
Wartawan: Katia Hetter
Isi Berita: 
A doctor warns that children's beverage choices significantly impact their long-term health.
High consumption of sugary drinks is linked to obesity, type 2 diabetes, and dental issues.
Water and milk are recommended as the healthiest options for children.
Parents should limit their kids' intake of soda, energy drinks, and artificially sweetened beverages.
