Judul: How companies can ease the strain on employees taking care of elderly parents or ill family members
Wartawan: Jeanne Sahadi
Isi Berita: 
Recognizing the challenges faced by employees with caregiving responsibilities, many employers are expanding support systems to promote work-life balance and overall well-being. Initiatives include offering flexible work schedules, providing paid family leave, and implementing on-site childcare services. These measures aim to alleviate the stress associated with caregiving duties, thereby enhancing employee satisfaction and productivity. By acknowledging and addressing the needs of caregivers, organizations foster a more inclusive and supportive workplace environment.
