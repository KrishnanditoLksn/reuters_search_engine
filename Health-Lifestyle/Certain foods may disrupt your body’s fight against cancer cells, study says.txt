Judul: Certain foods may disrupt your body’s fight against cancer cells, study says
Wartawan: Sandee LaMotte
Isi Berita: 
Summary: Recent studies have highlighted a concerning association between the consumption of ultraprocessed foods and omega-6 fatty acids with an elevated risk of colon cancer. Ultraprocessed foods, which make up nearly 70% of the U.S. food supply, contain additives like flavor enhancers, colors, and thickeners, and have been linked to various health issues, including cancer. Additionally, diets high in omega-6 fats, commonly found in processed snacks and fried foods, may disrupt the gut microbiome, leading to inflammation and increased cancer risk. Health experts recommend reducing the intake of these foods and incorporating more whole, minimally processed options to support overall health and reduce the risk of chronic diseases
