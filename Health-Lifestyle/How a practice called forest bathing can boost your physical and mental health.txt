Judul: How a practice called forest bathing can boost your physical and mental health
Wartawan:  Katia Hetter
Isi Berita: 
The election season can cause significant stress and anxiety, but managing it is essential for mental well-being. Experts recommend limiting news consumption, practicing mindfulness and relaxation techniques like meditation, staying physically active, and seeking support from family or friends. These strategies can help individuals maintain emotional balance and reduce anxiety during politically tense times.
